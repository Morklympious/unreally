# About Me
Welcome to this weird site you probably found on accident.

## What is this place?
This is a site I made to put all of my explorations or learnings. Unreal Engine does this really cute thing where they add some functionality and then don't say anything about it, and to be honest, it pisses me off just a little bit. 

I'll say that Unreal does a lot of great things. I'll also say that Unreal documents those things in such a poor way it caused me to spend a saturday standing up a blog in the hopes that both you and I might learn something. 

I used to have an [old blog about my learnings with javascript](https://stffrd.github.io), and I figured why not take a crack at another blog site that might help someone just like me, a fish out of water, learn better to swim in this ocean of the unknown.

## Okay. Fair. Who're you?
Hey!

My name is Bradley. On the internet I go by Morklympious.
